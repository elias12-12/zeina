About Us

Welcome to Zeina – A Legacy of Flavor and Family Nestled in the heart of Kfarsaroun, Zeina is not just a roastery — it’s a living legacy. Born from the hands of a passionate grandfather with a love for rich, authentic flavor, Zeina has grown into a beloved family business, now run by his children and soon, by their children too. At Zeina, you’ll find only the finest roasted nuts, from luxurious premium blends to the freshest everyday favorites — each batch slow-roasted to perfection. And for the coffee lovers? Our signature top-quality coffee is bold, smooth, and unforgettable — crafted to awaken your senses with every sip. More than a shop, Zeina is a tradition passed down through generations, where quality, warmth, and community come together in every bite and every cup.
